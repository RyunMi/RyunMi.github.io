Biography

Hello! I am a final-year Master student at Nanjing University of Science and Technology, under the supervision of Prof. Chen Gong (IET Fellow). I obtained my B.S. at Xiangtan University in 2023.

My research interests lie in the intersection of Domain Adaptation and Continual Learning. Now, I focus on exploring transferability and anti-forgetting ability of Large Multimodal Models in Open-world, with applications in multimodal areas.

Feel free to contact me if you are interested in discussing or collaborating with me.
